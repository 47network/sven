# Agent Multi-Tenancy

Tenant lifecycle management, member access control, quota enforcement and audit logging.

## Actions

### create-tenant
Provision a new tenant workspace with plan, quotas and owner.

### manage-members
Invite, update roles or remove members from a tenant.

### enforce-quotas
Check and enforce resource quotas for a tenant.

### send-invitation
Send an invitation to join a tenant with a specific role.

### audit-query
Query the tenant audit log with filters on actor, action or time range.

### upgrade-plan
Change a tenant plan and adjust associated quotas and limits.

### tenant-report
Generate a tenant utilization and health summary report.
