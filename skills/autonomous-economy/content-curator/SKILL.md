# Content Curator

Discovers, evaluates, and organizes content into curated collections optimized for quality, relevance, and diversity.

## Actions

### create-collection
Creates a new curated collection around a topic, with configurable quality and relevance thresholds.

### discover-content
Searches and evaluates content from configured sources, scoring for relevance and quality.

### curate-items
Selects the best items for a collection, balancing quality, freshness, and diversity.

### rank-collection
Re-ranks items within a collection based on updated scoring criteria or new content.

### publish-collection
Publishes a curated collection, making it available for consumption or marketplace listing.

### analyze-gaps
Identifies topics or perspectives missing from a collection for more comprehensive coverage.
