# Job Scheduler

Schedule and manage distributed jobs with retries, timeouts, and dependency tracking.

## Actions

### schedule-job
Create a new scheduled job with cron or interval expression.

### run-job
Immediately execute a scheduled job.

### pause-job
Pause a running or scheduled job.

### retry-failed
Retry failed job executions with configured policy.

### list-executions
List job execution history with filtering.

### analyze-performance
Analyze job execution performance and reliability metrics.
