# Taxonomy Builder

Creates, evolves, and maintains hierarchical taxonomy trees for organizing content, products, and knowledge.

## Actions

### build-taxonomy
Analyzes a corpus of content and automatically generates a hierarchical taxonomy structure.

### classify-entity
Assigns an entity to the most appropriate taxonomy node(s) with confidence scores.

### merge-nodes
Identifies and merges similar or duplicate taxonomy nodes based on semantic similarity thresholds.

### suggest-structure
Proposes taxonomy improvements: new categories, reorganizations, depth adjustments.

### export-taxonomy
Exports the taxonomy in standard formats (SKOS, JSON-LD, flat CSV, tree JSON).

### validate-coverage
Checks if the taxonomy adequately covers all content in the corpus, identifies gaps.
