# Trace Collector

OpenTelemetry-compatible distributed tracing collector with span analysis, bottleneck detection, and service dependency mapping.

## Actions

### collect-traces
Collect and store distributed trace spans from instrumented services.

### analyze-trace
Analyze a complete trace for bottlenecks, errors, and optimization opportunities.

### find-bottlenecks
Identify performance bottlenecks across service boundaries.

### service-map
Generate service dependency maps from trace data.

### compare-traces
Compare two traces to identify performance regressions or improvements.

### export-traces
Export trace data in OpenTelemetry, Jaeger, or Zipkin format.
