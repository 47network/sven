# Insight Extractor

Analyzes documents and data to extract structured insights, identify trends, discover patterns, and surface actionable recommendations.

## Actions

### extract-insights
Processes a document or dataset to extract key findings, trends, anomalies, and recommendations.

### connect-insights
Discovers relationships between extracted insights across multiple sources, building a knowledge graph.

### prioritize-insights
Ranks insights by actionability, confidence, and business impact.

### summarize-findings
Generates executive summaries from collections of extracted insights.

### track-trends
Monitors how insights evolve over time, identifying emerging or declining patterns.

### export-report
Compiles extracted insights into structured reports with supporting evidence.
