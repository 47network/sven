# Data Replicator

Automated data replication — cross-database sync, conflict resolution, lag monitoring, and failover replication.

## Actions

- **setup-replication**: Configure source-to-target replication with conflict resolution strategy
- **monitor-lag**: Track replication lag and alert on threshold breaches
- **resolve-conflicts**: Detect and resolve data conflicts between replicas
- **failover-promote**: Promote replica to primary during failover
