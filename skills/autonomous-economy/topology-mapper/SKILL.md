# Topology Mapper

Discovers and maps service topology, dependencies, and communication patterns across infrastructure.

## Actions

### discover-nodes
Scan and discover service nodes in the topology.

### map-edges
Map communication edges between discovered nodes.

### detect-changes
Detect topology changes since the last scan.

### analyze-dependencies
Analyze dependency chains and identify critical paths.

### export-map
Export the topology map in a structured format for visualization.

### check-health
Check health status of all nodes in the topology map.
