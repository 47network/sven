# DDoS Protector

Detects and mitigates DDoS attacks with configurable policies, challenge modes, and real-time traffic metrics.

## Actions

### create-policy
Create a DDoS protection policy with thresholds and mitigation mode.

### detect-attack
Analyze traffic patterns for DDoS indicators.

### mitigate-incident
Apply mitigation actions to an active DDoS incident.

### review-metrics
Review traffic metrics and attack history.
