# Permission Mapper

RBAC management — role hierarchy definition, permission mapping, access checks, and least-privilege analysis.

## Actions

- **map-roles**: Build complete role-permission matrix
- **check-access**: Verify if subject has permission for resource/action
- **find-excessive**: Identify overly permissive role assignments
- **optimize-hierarchy**: Recommend role hierarchy simplifications
