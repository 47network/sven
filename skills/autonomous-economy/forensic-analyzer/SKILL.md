# Forensic Analyzer

Digital forensics and incident investigation service with evidence collection, chain of custody tracking, timeline reconstruction, and comprehensive case management.

## Actions

### create-case
Open a new forensic investigation case with type, severity, and initial evidence.

### collect-evidence
Collect and preserve digital evidence with integrity hashing and chain of custody.

### analyze-timeline
Reconstruct event timelines from collected evidence and system logs.

### generate-findings
Produce investigation findings with supporting evidence references.

### close-case
Close an investigation case with final summary and lessons learned.

### export-case
Export complete case files including evidence, timeline, and findings.
